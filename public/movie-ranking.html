Top Film Recommendations

The Firm Favourites

Meet the Fockers

If you are searching for a film that never gets old, you've come to the right place.

This film follows, "Gaylord Focker," who is seeking approval from the in-laws. Only to find, the new father in law likes to do his research on the man her daughter is bringing home.

The film lines you up for some belly laughs and some heart felt momements. A must watch.

The Boat that Rocked

Do you sometimes feel feel like you were born in the wrong era?

Well..The Boat that Rocked is that type of film to make you wish you were born in the days of Pirate Radio.

If you want a film that is an easy watch, has an epic soundtrack and a brilliant cast then look no further.

Willie Wonka and the Chocolate Factory

Everyone has a go to family film, am i right?

What better way than to sit back, pile up the sweets, chocolate and popcorn and stick on every chocoholics dream.

Everyone loves films where we see an underdog come out on top.

The film follows charlie, a boy from humble beginnings, who gets the chance to visit the Eccentric Chocolate factory that is Willie Wonka's

There is Madness and Magic of Wonka is unmissable.
